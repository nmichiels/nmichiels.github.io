Raycasting Engine
Nick Michiels and Kenneth Devloo
Course: Computer Networks
Language: C++/Qt
[report (dutch)] [binary] [play now (IE only)]
[click here for more info/results]

Description

A raycasting engine and a powerful editor. Possibility to script the world.

Features:
   -Basic Raycasting Engine
   -High Resolution Textures with scaling resolution
   -Alpha-value Sprites
   -Editor to fully customize the scene
   -Interactive scene with scripting: triggering, events
   -Compass

Play Raycasting online (works only on Internet Explorer!)
You can download full maps here: maps (i.e. voorbeeldje.txt)
It can take a couple of minutes to load the app.
   -Key-Up: forward
   -Key-Down: backward
   -Key-Left: rotate left
   -Key-Right: rotate right
   -R: Look Up
   -F: Look Down
   -Space: Switch to mouse interaction
